How it works

Scan. Sip. See you soon.

STEP 1 — SCAN

A small card on the table

The card points your guest to a quick check-in page made for your place. First time here? We learn who took care of them. Been here before? We log the visit. Either way, they’re on your list and in this month’s drawing.

STEP 2 — SIP

The night carries on

Your guest scans, checks in, and goes right back to the glass, the conversation, the reason they came. No app. No pitch. No staff script. Like Old Friends takes it from there.

STEP 3 — SEE YOU SOON

A text that sounds like you

Days later, a warm note arrives from your place: personal, specific, and written in your voice. Not a coupon blast. An invitation back.

The doctrine

You do nothing. We mean it.

No portalNo dashboardNo staff trainingNo hardwareNo coupon blasts

People come back to places where somebody knows them. We built a service around that one true thing. We set it up, we write it, we send it, and once a month one page tells you exactly who came back.

Staff shout-outs

Guests tell us who took care of them, and the follow-up remembers it. That's the Cheers factor, working for you.

Google reviews, grown monthly

We ask your verified visitors — the right people, at the right moment — to say publicly what they already said at the table.

Slow-night rescue

When a Tuesday is dying, a pre-approved note goes to your nearby regulars. The fastest ad network in Savannah is the one that already loves you.

Locals and visitors, sorted

Savannah nights are two audiences. We know which is which, and we never text a tourist about Tuesday trivia.

The monthly basket

Every signup is an entry in a neighborhood prize drawing — a little occasion that keeps the list warm and the card scanned.

Friends of friends

Coming soon: endorsement exchanges with neighboring businesses you actually like. Your list hears it from you, theirs hears it from them.
